Popularity prediction of Youtube videos

My Final Year Project studied the relationship between the view counts (i.e., popularity) of Youtube videos and the sentiment fluctuation of the Youtuber in the same video in order to find out what are the key success factors to become even more popular Youtubers.
To know the sentiment fluctuation of Youtubers in their videos, I screenshotted videos every 5 seconds using Pafy, VLC, Argparse and use Real-time Convolutional Neural Networks for Emotion Classification to classify the facial emotion in each screenshot. I plotted the emotion line chart of each Youtube video by mapping the max value of each emotion classifications using matplotlib with the objective to correlate emotion fluctuation curves with view counts. I built a dataset with 3 channels 200 videos each, total 600 videos with emotion tags. Next phase of research I plan to train deep learning networks with this dataset to predict view counts.
